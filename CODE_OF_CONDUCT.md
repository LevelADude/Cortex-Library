# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment
We are committed to a respectful, harassment-free community for everyone, regardless of background, identity, experience level, or beliefs.

## Expected behavior
- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and provide actionable feedback.
- Focus discussion on code, product behavior, and evidence.

## Unacceptable behavior
- Harassment, personal attacks, or intimidation.
- Hate speech or discriminatory language.
- Deliberate disruption, trolling, or bad-faith engagement.
- Sharing private information without consent.

## Enforcement
Maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, or restrict participation for behavior that violates this policy.

## Reporting
Report concerns privately to: **conduct@cortex-library.dev**
